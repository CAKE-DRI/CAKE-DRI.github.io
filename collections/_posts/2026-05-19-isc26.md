[ISC High Performance 2026](https://isc-hpc.com/) is Europe's leading conference and exhibition for high performance computing (HPC), artificial intelligence, data analytics, and quantum computing. Taking place from **22–26 June 2026** at the Congress Center Hamburg in Germany, ISC brings together more than 3,500 scientists, engineers, researchers, and industry leaders from academia, government, and the private sector. Under this year's theme, *"Connecting the Dots"*, the programme spans five days of tutorials, keynotes, workshops, panels, posters, and one of Europe's largest HPC exhibitions, with over 200 international exhibitors.

CAKE is delighted to be taking an active part in ISC 2026. We will be presenting a **research poster** on better connecting and coordinating global digital research infrastructure, sharing space on an **exhibition booth** to meet the community and showcase the work of the UK DRI landscape, and **hosting a collaboration session** on sustainable collaboration and knowledge exchange across the UK and Europe. If you're heading to Hamburg, please do come and find us — we'd love to talk about how CAKE can help connect your project, community, or centre into the wider DRI ecosystem.
