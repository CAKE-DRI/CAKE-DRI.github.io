Are you interested in becoming Agile but not sure where to start?

Join us at the first ACIT Hub webinar to discover how Agile/Lean practices can be applied to add structure to workload management for research infrastructure engineers.

We will discuss what to consider before starting, how to choose an appropriate Agile/Lean solution, and the potential effects on your working practices.

During the webinar there will be an opportunity to ask questions so you can add to the discussion.
