The HPE Centre of Excellence is delivering a Workshop on Profiling and Optimisation and takes place online on Tue 14th Apr and Wed 15th Apr, from 09:00 to 17:00. The agenda is available on the [ARCHER2 website](https://www.archer2.ac.uk/training/courses/260414-hpe/Agenda.pdf)

Highlights:
- The course will cover the HPE tools available on Cirrus.
- The course will cover the HPE tools available on the ARCHER2 GPU nodes. Please note that these nodes will run with a new image that features ROCm 6.

More details and registration can be found on the [course webpage](https://www.archer2.ac.uk/training/courses/260414-hpe/)
