The VI-HPS Tuning Workshop will teach participants about performance analysis and diagnosis techniques, how to effectively use the VI-HPS tools suite, and provide hands-on experience with: Score-P, CUBE, Scalasca, Extra-P and MAQAO tools.

Participants are encouraged to bring their own MPI, OpenMP, or hybrid applications (including GPU codes) and will then receive expert coaching and optimisation advice based upon these. The workshop will run daily from 09:00 to 17:30 and make use of HPC Hardware Lab at Durham University , with accounts provided if needed.

See the [event website](https://shareing-dri.github.io/events/202604-VIHPS-workshop/) for more details and to sign up.
