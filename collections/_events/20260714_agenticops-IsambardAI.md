Isambard-AI provides an unprecedented AI research resource for the UK. Attracting thousands of researchers from academia, government and industry covering every conceivable research domain, users of the Isambard-AI do not always have a traditional HPC background. This diverse and innovative user community has exhibited unprecedented user patterns, heavily leveraging AI Agents and LLMs. This has presented novel challenges both in terms of technical guardrails and user guidance.

Join this webinar to discover an overview of our progress in developing 'Agentic Ops' to help protect the service and maximise user productivity.

During the webinar there will be an opportunity to ask questions.
