**Thursday 6th August, 15:00-16:30 (online)**

Following the UKRI National Compute Ecosystem Town Hall meeting in London on 2nd June 2026, UKRI is running this webinar to provide an opportunity for more people in the research and innovation community to engage with us on our plans for the Next National Supercomputing Service (NNSS) and the National Compute Resources (NCRs).

In this webinar, we will be sharing the slides that were presented in the town hall meeting and a panel Q&A, so there will be time for attendees to share their thoughts and ask questions. 

The webinar will be recorded and transcribed.
