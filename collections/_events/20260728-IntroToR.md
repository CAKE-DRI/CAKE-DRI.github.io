The **UKRI Digital Research Skills Catalyst** is running a two-day, hands-on workshop at the University of York and learn the foundations of reproducible data analysis using R and RStudio.

**What you will learn**

Through a combination of short presentations and live coding exercises, you will explore essential scientific computing concepts, including file management, data organisation, analysis workflows, and data visualisation. You will also gain practical experience importing, analysing, and presenting data in R, applying your new skills to biological datasets.

No previous programming experience is required.

**Is this for you?** We welcome researchers, technical staff, and postgraduate students in the life sciences who want to develop practical data analysis skills using R and RStudio. 

**Fee and scholarship info** Cost: £150/£200. 10 scholarships are available to support travel and accommodation costs. 

View the [website](https://digitalskillscatalyst.ac.uk/news.html) for more details or contact us [research-digital-skills@york.ac.uk](mailto:research-digital-skills@york.ac.u) for further information.
