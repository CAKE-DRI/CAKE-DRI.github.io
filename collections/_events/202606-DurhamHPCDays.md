The Durham HPC Days 2026 brings together researchers, developers, and practitioners from across the UK and beyond to explore the latest advances in high-performance computing. 

Over five days, the event offers a rich programme of keynote talks, technical sessions, and opportunities for discussion — alongside informal networking and social events designed to foster new collaborations.

Whether you are an experienced HPC user or just beginning to explore the field, Durham HPC Days provides a welcoming space to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and connect with the community.
