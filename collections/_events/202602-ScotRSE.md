​The first in-person meeting of the regional group for research software engineering in Scotland.

​With funding from the Software Sustainability Institute, lunch will be provided and there is a small pot to support travel. Please register by 3 February for food or travel support.

​Programme to be announced, but the meeting aims to:

​- Develop the community of RSEs and associated technical professionals in Scotland;
- Explore collaboration opportunities across institutions in Scotland

​Register [here](https://luma.com/rpnv7vjo0) and for more information about ScotRSE see [https://scotrse.github.io/](https://scotrse.github.io/)
