**HPC, AI, Quantum: Powering Innovation and Sustainability**

ISC 2026 connects scientists, engineers, and technology leaders to explore the future of high performance computing. 
We will examine today’s breakthroughs in artificial intelligence, high performance computing and quantum technologies, as well as what lies ahead.

## UK involvement in ISC

The UK HPC and DRI community is well represented across the ISC 2026 programme. Below is a day-by-day summary of sessions featuring UK-based speakers, contributors, and chairs, drawn from the published ISC programme. Click any session title to view its full details on the ISC planner.
