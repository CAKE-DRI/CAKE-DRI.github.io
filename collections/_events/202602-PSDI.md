Starting in February 2026, the DCC is launching a series of webinars to showcase the digital research infrastructure projects and communities in the U.K. and beyond in collaboration with UK Research and Innovation (UKRI). Join us to learn about state-of-the-art initiatives that aim to advance research tools and techniques, foster collaboration and data sharing, and accelerate discoveries across a range of academic disciplines and sectors.

Join us from 14:00-15:00 GMT on 4th February 2026 for the first webinar in the series 'Getting to know the Physical Sciences Data Infrastructure (PSDI) which is free to join and open to all. Dr Cerys Willoughby and Dr Samantha Pearman-Kanza of University of Southampton will introduce the Physical Sciences Data Infrastructure (PSDI) platform, resources, and community, outline ways to get involved, and answer questions from the audience.
