The **UKRI Digital Research Skills Catalyst** is hosting a free online Metatranscriptomics Workshop running from **11 to 22 May 2026**.

The workshop explores advanced methods in metatranscriptomic data analysis, covering experimental design through to interpretation and visualisation of results. It is aimed at researchers working with microbial community RNA-seq data and others looking to develop their bioinformatics skills in this area.

**Cost:** Free

**Format:** Online

**More information:** [digitalskillscatalyst.ac.uk/news.html](https://digitalskillscatalyst.ac.uk/news.html)

For questions, contact the Catalyst team at [research-digital-skills@york.ac.uk](mailto:research-digital-skills@york.ac.uk).
