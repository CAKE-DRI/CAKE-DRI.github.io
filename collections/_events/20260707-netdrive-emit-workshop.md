The purpose of the workshop is to gather and discuss ideas about best practice for the measurement and effective use 
of carbon emissions data across scientific computing job roles. Job roles may include users of compute services, 
managers of compute services, software engineers, computational scientists, project/programme funders, and more. 
We are interested in hearing a range of perspectives, from across academia and research organisations.
