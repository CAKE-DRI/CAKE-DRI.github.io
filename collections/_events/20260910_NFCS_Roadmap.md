The National Federated Compute Service (NFCS) NetworkPlus Reporting Conference brings together the outcomes, insights, and recommendations from 26 pioneering Flexible Fund projects shaping the future of Digital Research Infrastructure (DRI) federation in the UK.

Over these two days, our speakers will present the evidence, community perspectives, and strategic recommendations emerging from one of the most comprehensive investigations into UK-wide DRI federation ever undertaken.

Join project leaders, researchers, and invited experts as they explore:

* The key recommendations for future DRI federation
* The evidence and findings underpinning the roadmap
* The projected impact across research communities and institutions
* Real-world experiences from existing and emerging federated communities
