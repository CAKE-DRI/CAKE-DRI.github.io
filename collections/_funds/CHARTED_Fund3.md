This project offers flexible funding opportunities to support relevant activities and collaborations. It supports the development of tools and frameworks that improve the navigability of the ecosystem, including the design, creation, or testing of resources such as self-assessment tools, navigation aids, approaches to continuous professional development, and best practice guidance. Expressions of interest are invited, with the possibility of combining complementary proposals. The fund totals £350k, supporting small projects of up to approximately £5k and larger projects of up to £150k.
