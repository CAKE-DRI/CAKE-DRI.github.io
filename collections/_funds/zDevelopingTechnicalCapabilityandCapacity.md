This project provides placement and training opportunities through targeted skills development activities. Developing Technical Capability and Capacity for UK Secure Data Environments has delivered student placement opportunities to build capability in secure data environments. While future placement calls are not currently advertised, the 2026 retreat (11–13 November 2026) is aimed at Early Career Research Technical Professionals (the event is fully funded (incl. workshop/accommodation/full board)) and offers an opportunity to develop skills, exchange knowledge, and engage with the wider community working in secure data infrastructure.
