This project offers flexible funding opportunities to support relevant activities and collaborations. UK RAS STEPS supports knowledge exchange placements that enable Research Technical Professionals working in robotics and autonomous systems to gain practical experience, build collaborations, and share best practices with leading laboratories and facilities across the UK. Funding covers travel, accommodation, subsistence, and related placement costs, supporting visits ranging from one day to one month or longer where justified. These placements aim to strengthen professional networks, encourage knowledge transfer, and enhance technical expertise across the robotics and autonomous systems community.
