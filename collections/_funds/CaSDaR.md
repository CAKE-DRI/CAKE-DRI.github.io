The CaSDaRs Flexible Fund supports data stewardship across the United Kingdom. It aims to advance careers and skills for data stewards and build evidence of their value across disciplines and organisations. It offers funding calls, bursaries, workshop support, and ongoing funding streams for events and related applications.
