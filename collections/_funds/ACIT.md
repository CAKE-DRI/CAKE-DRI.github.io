The Accelerated Compute Infrastructure Training Hub at the University of Surrey strengthens the United Kingdom workforce running advanced computing. It aims to coordinate and elevate national skills. It offers recognised training and events on accelerators, high-performance networking, storage, orchestration, hybrid cloud, infrastructure automation, security, sustainability, and researcher support.
