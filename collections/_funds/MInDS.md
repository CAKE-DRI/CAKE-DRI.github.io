This project offers flexible funding opportunities to support relevant activities and collaborations. The Mathematical Innovators in the Digital Space (MInDS) Flexible Fund (2025–2029) supports the expansion of the UK Innovation Research Associate (InRA) community by enabling university mathematical sciences departments to develop InRA capacity. This funding stream aims to strengthen capability, foster collaboration, and broaden participation in innovation-focused research roles across the UK mathematical sciences landscape.
