This project offers funding opportunities to support the organisation of retreats (there is a rolling deadline). CAKE retreats reduce barriers to collaboration by providing financial support that enables individuals to organise retreats that strengthen the future of the DRI landscape. These opportunities are designed to build new partnerships and foster collaborations that continue beyond the placement or visit.
