The MUSCAT platform at Queen Mary University of London addresses the shortage of skilled professionals in antenna measurement and radio-frequency characterization. Led by Dr Max Munoz Torrico and colleagues, it offers bespoke training and workshops, access to the Antennas Measurement Lab, and industry partnerships to tackle challenges and accelerate commercialization.
