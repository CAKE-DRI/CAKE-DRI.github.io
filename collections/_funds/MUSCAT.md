(TEMP. Mark - Cannot find web)This project provides training opportunities through recognised events and skills development activities. The MUSCAT platform at Queen Mary University of London addresses the shortage of skilled professionals in antenna measurement and radio-frequency characterisation by offering bespoke training and workshops, access to the Antennas Measurement Laboratory, and collaboration with industry partners to tackle technical challenges and support innovation and commercialisation in this specialised field.
